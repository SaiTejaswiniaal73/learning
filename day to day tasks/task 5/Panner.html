Paneer Butter

"Paneer Butter Masala" is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a tomato-based gravy with butter and mild spices The word "paneer" refers to the soft, non-melting cheese, while "butter masala" signifies a buttery, spiced gravy. This dish is known for its velvety texture and mildly sweet, savory flavor, making it a popular choice in Indian cuisine, especially when served with naan, roti, or rice.
